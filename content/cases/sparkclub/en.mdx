## The problem

Companies don't lose talent over salary — they lose it over disconnection. SparkClub wanted to solve employee disengagement with data-driven, AI-powered experiences. The challenge: communicate a complex idea to investors before having a single real user.

## What I designed

A functional MVP for three user types with non-overlapping workflows: **Team Leads/HR** for oversight and analytics, **Employees** to discover and join experiences, **Vendors** to coordinate and manage activities.

The platform uses AI to recommend experiences based on team health signals, detect disengagement before it becomes a problem, and personalize communication for each employee.

<ImagePlaceholder src="/assets/cases/sparkclub/spark.png" />

## The process

I started by mapping all three journeys in parallel — the risk was designing three separate products that felt bolted together. The solution was a shared Design System in Figma from day one: same components, same state logic, same color system.

Weeks 1–2 were pure information architecture and flows. Weeks 3–5, high fidelity. Week 6, the interactive prototype that became the pitch deck.

Complete interactive prototype, Design System with 60+ components, and architecture ready for development handoff.
